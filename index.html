About the Brand

Kavari Polo represents a new generation of polo — where heritage meets modern identity. Inspired by the spirit of the horse, the precision of the sport, and the lifestyle surrounding it, this brand is built for those who live with intent, confidence, and quiet power.

Founder

Kavin Gaur, a polo player and visionary, created Kavari Polo to redefine how polo is represented. Combining discipline from the sport with a modern mindset, his mission is to build a globally recognized equestrian brand.

Philosophy

  • Discipline over hype
  • Performance meets elegance
  • Built for legacy, not trends

Future Vision

Kavari Polo aims to expand into premium apparel, riding gear, and global collaborations. The goal is to position itself as a luxury equestrian lifestyle brand representing modern polo culture worldwide.
